Welcome, Challengers!

Get ready to face off in a classic battle of wits. The Tic-Tac-Toe board awaits!

A quick reminder of the basics before you play!

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Two players take turns placing their marks, X or O, on an empty square.
  • The first player to get three of their marks in a row—either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally—wins the game.
  • If all nine squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a draw.
